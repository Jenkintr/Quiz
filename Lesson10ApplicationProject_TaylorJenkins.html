Lesson 10:

My Hobby: Painting

I enjoy painting. I have painted since the age of 5 years old. I paint with anything from watercolors to oil paints. Because of my love for art, I pursued a Bachelors Degree in Fine Arts where I took many painting classes. During my studies I learned ALOT of information about painting.

Upon my first day of class I stocked up on the basics: paintbrushes, painter's palettes, canvases, and paints. If you don't know anything about painting, the choices are overwhelming when it comes to buying supplies. The most common shaped brushes are flats, filberts, brights, and rounds. These brushes are numbered on the handle to indicate the size. When using oil paint, canvas is the typical surface to paint on, however "form follows function" so it really just depends on what your goal is. Some artist paint on wood with their oil paints, but that requires alot of gesso.

You obviously need paints to paint... the basic oil paints that you should have in your toolbox are: Titanium White, Ivory Black, Cadmium Red, Alizarin Crimson, Ultramarine Blue, Pthalo Blue, Cadmium Yellow, and Cadmium Yellow Light. If you have these primary colors, you can mix ANY color you'll ever need by squirting a dab of paint on your palette and using your palette knife to mix the colors. For example, if you want a green, just mix Cadmium yellow and Ivory Black. It creates a very earthy grean as opposed to mixing blue and yellow.

Once you are done painting, you will have to clean up. You will need turpintine (a paint thinner) and a razor blade so that you can scrape off the excess paint on your palette. You will also need paper towels and a fire-proof trashcan (turpintine will spontaneously combust when on balled up paper towels). Once everything is clean, you will need to put the painting someplace safe where it can dry.It takes oil paint about 3 weeks to dry depending on how thickly you applied the paint. After the painting is dry, you can hang it up to enjoy forever... or enjoy until you don't enjoy it anymore. Then you can just paint over it or sell it.
